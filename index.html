poster designs.

dsgn 1526 - thesis.

This is my progress on my thesis research posters. I have been exploring childhood emotional neglect and learning of ways to overcome this form of neglect in adulthood. This project consists of a series of 3 posters. The first one will educate people on childhood emotional neglect. The second poster displays the aftermath in adulthood and what the symptoms may be presented as. The third a final poster will explore solutions and go into a little bit about my design solution specifically.

Did you know that more than 18% of children are emotionally neglected. That is about 1 in 5 kids. It is likely that it is not reported and therefore not addressed or treated. This means that these children are suffering in silence.

As a result of being unnoticed and being left untreated, there is a cycle that forms and repeats until it is broken. My goal in this thesis project is to stop the cycle of generational neglect and trauma.
